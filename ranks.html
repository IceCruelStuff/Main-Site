Our Ranks

Read the section below to find out information about our rankings.

Rankings. Every major server has them, so why wouldn't we? Rank upgrades are earned by trust. The regular rank upgrades go up to the Titan rank. Once you have reached the Titan rank, you may email mariobrostech@gmail.com, and send a CC to remotevase@gmail.com, or start a Private Conversation with mario_bros_tech or masteremotevase on the forums.. This email must answer all of the requirements in the staff application page. If your application is good enough, then we will promote you. If we do not feel that you are ready, we will tell you when you may submit another application. Unfortunately, some people may not even be close to good enough, and so they may not even have any other chances to submit an application. Do not spam the applications, because if you do, you will be demoted. We will be keeping track of who submitted applications, and when they submitted it, along with when we told them to submit another application, and if they submit another application before the date that we told them, then they will be demoted. Staff ranks have responsibilities such as helping admins, or checking for hackers. Thanks for understanding! A full list of ranks can be found below.

RANKS + PERKS

[Member]- Always in the default gamemode, no special perks. Has the default chat wait time (10 seconds between messages).
[Starter]- Always (excluding hub server) in whatever gamemode the admin places them in. Has a 7.5 second chat wait time.
[Builder]- Can switch between the default gamemode & creative, 5 second chat wait time. Creative mode with MANY restrictions. Any player who has earned this rank will be demoted to the Starter rank while in the factions server, as being able to build in the factions server would make you too overpowered.
[Titan]- Can switch between adventure, creative and survival, 2 second chat wait time. Creative mode with some restrictions. Players with this rank will loose the permission to be in creative mode while they are in the factions server, as being able to build in the factions server would make you too overpowered.
[Helper - Staff]- Can switch between all gamemodes, 1 second chat wait time. Creative mode with a few restrictions. Responsibility: A moderator/admin will say what the helper must do, and the you MUST do it, or else you will be demoted.
[Moderator - Staff]- Can switch between all gamemodes, no chat wait time, Creative mode a few restrictions. Responsibility: At intervals of every 25-30 minutes, fly around the server that you are assigned to, looking around thoroughly for hackers/broken blocks, and report everything to the admins via email/the forums.
[Super Moderator - Staff]- Can switch between all gamemodes, no chat wait time, Almost no Creative mode restrictions. Responsibility: At intervals of every 5-10 minutes, fly around the server that you are assigned to, looking around thoroughly for hackers/broken blocks, and report everything to the admins via email/the forums.
[Administrator]- The only admins in the entire network are mario_bros_tech, masteremotevase, and Kylosaurus. They have access to everything.
